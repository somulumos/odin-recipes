Chili

Description

This recipe is for vegan chili. It is a delicious, hearty meal to share in the colder months. I never tire of making or eating this chili.

Ingredients

  • onions
  • garlic
  • tomatoes
  • cooked beans
  • bell peppers
  • a hot pepper
  • carrots
  • celery
  • olive oil
  • spices: cumin, coriander, chili powder, cayenne, salt, pepper

Steps

  1. Heat a splash of olive oil in a large pot on the stove.
  2. Chop onions, bell peppers, and garlic - add to pot.
  3. Add spices and stir frequently, letting vegetables soften and caramelize.
  4. Chop carrots and celery - add to pot and stir.
  5. Chop tomatoes - add to pot and stir.
  6. Add beans to pot, stir, cover the pot, reduce heat and simmer for at least an hour to combine flavors.
  7. Enjoy!
