Fried Veggies

Description

This is a delicious and nutritious recipe for fried vegetables, known in Indian cuisine as Vegetable Pakora.

This recipe is simple, yet satisfying. Best eaten fresh and hot!

Ingredients

  • vegetables (of your choosing)
  • salt
  • cumin
  • coriander
  • any other desired spices
  • flour
  • egg (optional)
  • oil for frying

Steps

  1. Finely chop or grate vegetables, place in large bowl.
  2. Generously sprinkle salt on veggies and mix, leave to sit about 10 minutes.
  3. Add flour, optional egg, and spices, mix until veggies well coated with batter.
  4. On stove, heat oil in pot or pan deep enough for frying.
  5. Scoop spoonfuls of battered veggies into hot oil, fry on each side until brown.
  6. Remove and drain over towel, repeat until all veggies fried.
  7. Serve hot with preferred chutney or dipping sauces.
