Pizza

Description

This is a basic homemade pizza. A delicious and hearty meal with toppings of your choosing.

Ingredients

  • flour
  • yeast
  • warm water
  • salt
  • olive oil
  • pizza sauce
  • cheese
  • toppings

Steps

  1. In large mixing bowl, mix yeast and warm water until frothy.
  2. Add flour, pinch of salt, and olive oil, knead until thoroughly mixed.
  3. Coat bowl in olive oil, roll dough into a ball in the bowl, cover with a cloth, and let sit for 30 minutes.
  4. Preheat oven to 500 degrees.
  5. When dough has risen, roll out flat onto pizza pan.
  6. Add sauce, cheese, and toppings as desired.
  7. Bake for 15-20 minutes, or until cheese is melted and crust is browned.
